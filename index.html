About Me

Following a redundancy, I decided to start a new career based around my love of chocolate. I trained extensively for this role and practised for many hours every day. I sourced the chocolate from local chocolatiers following vast amounts of research and testing.

I take my new job very seriously and I hope my passion and love for chocolate shines through.

How it Works

Upon arrival, you will be given short demonstration on the origins of chocolate and how it is made. Along with an overview of the different types of chocolate available and the tastes and smells you should be looking for. Following a short tasting demonstration, you will be given an apron and assigned to your tasting area. You will be provided with plenty of water, hand washing facilities and notepaper and pen to record your findings.

I have a selection of chocolate items available for purchase following the tasting session. You may wish to record you findings on the dedicated section of my website and compare these to your fellow chocolate tasters.

I hope you enjoy your time with me and I hope to see you again soon.
